Biography

Multimodal AI Researcher for Structured Evidence, Memory, and Agents

I study how AI systems recover, organize, audit, and act on evidence from noisy multimodal observations.

My work progresses from document structure to actionable world structure: from multimodal parsing, evidence graphs, and reliability diagnostics to structured memory and planning mechanisms for future agents.
