Research Focus

My research currently spans three connected themes: structured evidence, reliability diagnostics, and emerging directions in structured memory and agent planning. Right now, I am extending hierarchical document retrieval into actionable memory and executable planning abstractions for future multimodal agents.

Structured Evidence and Retrieval

Established
