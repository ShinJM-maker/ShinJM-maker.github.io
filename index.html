Biography

Multimodal Researcher for Unstructured Data

I am Joongmin Shin, a Multimodal AI Researcher dedicated to unlocking the value of complex, unstructured data (e.g., Documents, Raw text, Robotics data, Sequential data). Bridging natural language processing and computer vision, I focus on transforming long, noisy documents and other complex inputs into searchable, explainable, and highly structured formats. My research connects advanced multimodal parsing, structure-aware chunking, and robust Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipelines, empowering QA systems to deliver precise, evidence-grounded answers for real-world applications.
