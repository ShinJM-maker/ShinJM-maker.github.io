Senior Researcher / Research Scientist, Korea University Founder & Lead, KUDoc — Korea University Document AI Research Team

From unstructured inputs to structured evidence,
and from document structure to dynamic world structure.

I research how AI systems recover, organize, and audit evidence from heterogeneous unstructured observations, with a current focus on structure-aware evidence systems for documents and long-context reasoning.

As the Founder & Lead of KUDoc, I direct team-based research projects and build collaborative programs with partners at Konkuk and Soongsil.

Looking ahead, I aim to extend this foundation toward structured memory, world models, and planning for future agents.

Research Leadership

KUDoc is the Document AI research team I founded and lead within Korea University’s NLP & AI Lab.

It serves as a collaborative research platform for document understanding, multimodal RAG, evaluation, and applied AI systems, allowing me to define directions, organize team-based projects, and connect foundational methods with sustained research output and real-world deployment.
